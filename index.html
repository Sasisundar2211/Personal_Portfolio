The Product

FirmRunner
Pre-Seed · Active Build

A 5-person accounting firm loses roughly $90,000 a year to work no one bills for. FirmRunner is built to close that gap.

30% of every working hour in accounting is non-billable. That is not a calculation problem. It is an operations problem. FirmRunner ships five pre-configured AI agents that handle the work no one wants to do but every firm pays for. Firms get running in under 10 minutes. No API keys. No technical setup. No IT team required.

Pricing: $199 to $499 per month per firm. 10-question onboarding. Zero setup friction.
